REMINDER - TRANSIT NOTICE - LINE 3 TTC:

TTC reminds customers that there will be no service this weekend on Line 3 - Scarborough RT between Kennedy and McCowan station due to track and infrastructure work. Shuttle buses will operate for the duration of the closure.

Kennedy, Lawrence East and Scarborough Centre stations will remain open for the sale of fares. Ellesmere, Midland and McCowan stations will be closed.

SHOOTING - NORTH YORK:

Toronto Police are investigating after a male teenager was shot several times at a townhouse complex on Rochefort Dr, in the area of Don Mills Rd and Eglinton Ave E in North York. The teenager suffered serious injuries and was taken to hospital via emergency run.

No suspect description available at this time. Anyone with information is asked to contact Toronto Police 54 Division at 416-808-5400.

SHOOTING - SCARBOROUGH

Toronto Police are investigating after a male was grazed by three bullets following a shooting that took place at around 9:30pm yesterday in the area of Victoria Park Ave and Finch Ave E in Scarborough.

Police responded to calls for a shooting at that time and located shell casings but no victim. The victim later appeared in hospital saying he was grazed by bullets. His injuries were not life-threatening.

POLICE SEARCHING FOR PERSONS WITH A GUN - SCARBOROUGH:

Toronto Police are searching for three male suspects, one which was seen with a rifle, in the area of Corporate Dr and Lee Centre Dr (major intersection of McCowan Rd and Highway 401) in Scarborough.

Police say the males were wearing masks and fleeing the scene on foot. No injuries reported. Anyone with information is asked to contact Toronto Police 42 Division at 416-808-4200.
